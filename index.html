# CV

Yong ZHANG is an associate professor of Beijing National Research Center for Information Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, a committee member of Information System Society of CCF, a senior member of China Computer Federation, and a member of IEEE and ACM. He received his BSc degree in Computer Science and Technology in 1997, and PhD degree in Computer Software and Theory in 2002 from Tsinghua University. From 2002 to 2005, he did his Postdoc at Cambridge University, UK.

He has published over 200 articles in high-impact journals or conferences such as TKDE, TOIS, VLDBJ, IPM, IoT, WWWJ, HISC, SIGMOD, VLDB, ICDE, AAAI, SIGIR, WWW, ACL, IJCAI, CIKM and EMNLP. He holds 27 patents of invention and 25 software copyrights. He has served as a reviewer for many journals and conferences, such as TKDD, IJIM, ACM TMIS, KBS, IPM, WWWJ, ESWA, CMPB, BDR, CMBJ, IMU, CI, FCS, ACI, HISS, KDD, AAAI, ACL, EMLP, ICDE, DASFAA, DSAA, WAIM/ApWeb, ICSH, COMPSAC, ADMA, HealthCom and SEKE. He is the associate editor of “Health Information Science and Systems” and “Journal of Social Computing”.
